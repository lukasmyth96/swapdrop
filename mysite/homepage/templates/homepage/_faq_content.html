Swapdrop is a social clothes swapping platform where you can renew your wardrobe by swapping your clothes with other users.

Once you’ve uploaded items you can begin the search for a swap. You can explore other users' items through our feed and make a swap offer on any item you like. Our users decide which offers to make, accept and reject.

Swapping is an easy, inexpensive, fun and environmentally friendly way for you to renew your wardrobe. At Swapdrop we believe in creating a world where your wardrobe is constantly evolving.

Yes! Swapping is a great way to change your unsustainable fashion consumption habits. Every time you swap instead of buying something new you help reduce your carbon footprint and the enormous water requirements in the production of each new item of clothing. Our courier system and packaging are also both carbon neutral, with our mailing bags made out of recyclable sugar cane.

Once a match has been made both users have 48 hours to complete the checkout process. You will simply be required to enter your address and choose a pick-up time. We will take the swap payment at the point of pick-up.

At the moment we can only accommodate a 1 for 1 swap per transaction. This means that only one given item can be swapped for any other given single item on our site.
