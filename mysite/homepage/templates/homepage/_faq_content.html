During check out we’ll ask you to choose a pick-up slot. Before the pick-up slot you should make sure you have cleaned your item and have it ready to pass to our delivery rider. You should then place your item in the box, step back and our rider will take the box. Our rider will then take your payment via card payment or cash.

At the moment we can only accommodate a 1 for 1 swap. This means that only one given item can be swapped for any other given single item on our site.

Your payment includes the picking up of your item at your door and the delivery of the swap to both individuals. We also include our service fee within your payment.
