We define a “Swap” to constitute a one-for-one exchange of two items of clothes, shoes or other accepted items, between two individuals (users) without monetary exchange. 

We define a “Feed” to constitute as a presented grid of images of users items with the availability to view information on any item on the feed and make an “Offer” to any item as seen on the feed.

We define a “Live” state of an item to be when the owner of an item has not yet accepted any offer on the given item.


We define a “Matched” state of an item to be when an owner of an item has accepted an offer on the given item but both users within the swap have not yet checked out.
